The Grad Corner

A guide to higher studies — UG Wiki

We recognize that many students choose to pursue higher studies, and applying to them is an important process that can be quite confusing to navigate. Thus, we intend to ease this crucial process with this booklet and help you prepare for it. Here, we provide you with information about applying to universities — right from deciding where to go for higher studies to building an application, and the requirements for the same.
