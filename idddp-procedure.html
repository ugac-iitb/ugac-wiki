7Application Procedure

IDDDP

  1. Find a guide/supervisor for your IDDDP project
  2. SOP, course plan, research credentials (optional)
  3. Completely online application – choose up to three departments/programs and two specializations (if applicable)
  4. Apply via External ASC → [Forms/Requests] → IDDDP Application

Here’s a pdf guide for applying through the external asc portal:

https://portal.iitb.ac.in/asc/IDDDP.pdf

Approvals required from:

  • Faculty Adviser
  • Convener, DUGC of the Home Department
  • Convener, DUGC/DPGC of the Host Department
  • Final decision by UGAPEC

All approvals are fully online via Internal ASC

For more information, refer to the IDDDP Guidelines 2025

Dual Degree Program (guidelines vary by department)

  1. Find a guide/supervisor for your DD project
  2. Get the application form from your department office/through your DGSec
  3. SOP, course plan, research credentials(optional)
  4. Get it signed by your faculty advisor and guide(not required for some departments)
  5. Submit it to the DUGC through the department office/DGSec
NOTE:
  • Your entry into the IDDDP Program (even after getting selected) will remain provisional till 8th semester, i.e till the point you complete your entire B.S and B.Tech curriculum
  • The payment of your TAship (if selected) will be subject to terms and conditions as applicable to usual DD programs time to time
  • You will get a new faculty advisor after selection from the host department and your roll number remains the same
  • In general, based on the feedback of students we have received, since the program is new, the processes related to registration of courses, tagging etc, can get a bit complicated and time consuming. But it is a general consensus that this factor should not play a very heavy weight in deciding your decision for this program
