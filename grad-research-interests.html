Narrowing Down Research Interests

Identify Departments

Focus on departments that align with your career goals.

Learning from Seniors

Gain insights from seniors who have successfully figured out their research interests.

Explore Professors' Work

Research the publications and work of professors at potential universities. Align your interests with professors' research areas.

Networking

Connect with alumni and professionals to understand the field's demand and scope.

Explore Research Areas

Use online resources and academic papers. Dive into both established and emerging research topics.

Take Extra Courses

Enroll in courses outside your prescribed curriculum — especially in your 3rd and 4th years.

Projects & Internships

Participate in hands-on experiences. Programs like SURP provide exposure to various fields.
