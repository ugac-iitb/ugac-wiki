Welcome to UG Wiki

Finding the right courses, minors, and academic opportunities can be challenging. UG Wiki brings together information, resources, and student experiences to help you explore the options available at IIT Bombay and make informed academic decisions.

Whether you're planning your next semester, exploring a new field, or looking for insights from students who have taken a course before, we hope this serves as a useful starting point.

Happy exploring!
